About The Project

Welcome to my multimodal Advanced Writing reflection!

If you head on over to the main tab (Home Page), you should see all of the learning goals in grey boxes. If you click one, a bunch of resources will load to help you complete that goal. The Unit papers provide plenty of opportunities to meet - or exceed - the learning goals. The challenge is doing it write (lol).

The second tab of this website is designed to inspire you (RSS Inspiration). With every click of the shuffle button, 30 new news articles will appear. Those are updated live and will be new each time and each day. They’re sourced from about 21 different RSS feeds from several news sources. Feel free to checkout the README in the source code to see the exact links.

This is similar to the process that I used to work through my Unit papers, and I survived the class (knock on wood). Good luck with your semester!
